Welcome to our website

Where various art forms converge.

We are three Turkish university students studying in France, united by our passion for art and literature. Our goal is to share this passion with you and, among other things, to introduce the beauty of Turkish literature to Turkish, francophone and anglophone audiences.

Here, with our friends, we translate Turkish poetry into English and French and create original content, including poems, short stories, articles and essays about various topics such as literary analysis, photo galleries and much more. With the help of our circle of friends who have similar passion and enthusiasm towards art, we wish to make consuming, creating and publishing art more accessible to you.
