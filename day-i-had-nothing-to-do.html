The Day I Had Nothing To Do

Başak Ünal

I woke up early today

Gotta get up and start the day

Only loser idiots can stay

In the bed the whole day

The first week of my holiday

The weather is even gray

Can’t be a reason to stay

Lying in bed and play

What would my dad say

If he saw me delay

Sure to get up and repay

Or else it would be a betray

Gotta spend a good day

Maybe clean the driveway

“Don’t wanna be a stray”

To God this is how I pray

A stomachache they say

How is it a holy hooray?

Perfect excuse to stay

In my bed the whole day
